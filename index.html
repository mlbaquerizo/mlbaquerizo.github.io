Yonder helps pastoralist communities around the world preserve their livelihood, by providing them access to information and resources (e.g grass land, water sources, safe routes) to prepare and support their migration journey.
The objective of the project is to design a working prototype for a mobile/web application which provides pastoralist communities with information on land monitoring: water availability, grassland, and assist communities in networking.
We developed 3 possible mediums to accommodate the widest possible range of users : A web app, a mobile app, and an SMS-based service. Our solutions are deliberately lo-fi because of the likelihood of slow internet connections or none at all.
Technologies:

  • Waterpoint Data Exchange (Socrata) for data on water sources.
  • Weather Underground API for weather data and alerts.
  • Twilio API for SMS service.

Yonder was awarded the prize for "Greatest Galactic Impact" at NASA Space Apps Challenge 2016 NYC.
